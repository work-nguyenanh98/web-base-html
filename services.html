Services at About Burgers

A Cherished Upper Peninsula Tradition Since 1934

Simple, Delicious, and Worth the Wait

At Out and About Burgers, we keep things straightforward: bold, flavorful burgers made fresh and fast, served from a humble trailer—but with a whole lot of heart. Whether you're a loyal local or just discovering us for the first time, we offer a no-frills experience with food that speaks for itself.

Drive-Thru Convenience, Made Fresh to Order

We operate as a drive-thru-only kiosk, which means everything is geared for quick pickup without compromising quality. You'll place your order at the window, loop through the lot, and receive your food hot, fresh, and made just for you. Even when there's a line, we keep it moving fast—and our regulars say the wait is always worth it.

Our Signature Menu

Our menu may be simple, but it's crafted to perfection. Here's what we're known for:

  • Burgers: Juicy, flavorful patties served single, double, or triple with cheese. Try it with our signature Cagney sauce or go all in with grilled onions and melted cheese "Cagney-style."
  • Fries: Crispy and generously portioned. Choose classic, sweet potato, or fully loaded.
  • Onion Rings: Thick-battered, golden, and crispy—these are a fan favorite.
  • Grilled Cheese: A buttery classic for those who want something simple and satisfying.
  • Shakes & Malts: Creamy, hand-spun shakes and malts in rotating seasonal flavors. Always thick enough to dunk your fries.

Affordable Comfort Food

We believe a great meal shouldn't break the bank. Many guests are pleasantly surprised by how filling and affordable our portions are—especially when compared to national chains. Families, students, and solo diners alike all appreciate our generous servings and wallet-friendly prices.

What Makes Us Special

We're not just another burger spot. Out and About Burgers has built a loyal following thanks to:

  • Flavor that rivals In-N-Out (some even say it's better!)
  • Friendly staff and fast service, even during peak hours
  • Gluten-free bun options and customizations like lettuce wraps
  • Pet-friendly atmosphere — bring your pup along while you wait
  • Locally loved shake-of-the-month flavors and a touch of community personality in everything we do

Accessibility & Comfort

Our setup is drive-thru only, but we ensure an easy experience for everyone. The space is wheelchair accessible, with ample parking and clearly marked flow for cars. Most guests enjoy their food in the car, take it home, or stop nearby for a quick picnic meal.

Come As You Are

Out and About is casual, fun, and designed to deliver great food, fast. Whether you're swinging by for a quick lunch, late dinner, or a snack between errands, we're always ready to serve up something memorable.

Stop by. Get fed. Be happy.
