Craving birria in San Antonio?

  • You've got two mouth watering options with Birria Barrio—each serving the juicy, cheesy, crispy tacos you love, with some menu differences that make each spot unique.

What Both Locations Serve

Both S. Presa St and S. Flores St locations offer the core Birria Barrio favorites:

  • Quesabirria Tacos (3) with consomé $11.00
  • El Chavo Tacos (3) $13.99
    (with crispy chicharrón)
  • Birria Tacos (3) $10.99
  • Birria Ramen $11.99
  • Grilled Cheese con consomé $12.00
  • Keto Taco (1) $6.99
  • Party Tray (20 Quesabirria Tacos + consomé) $74.99+

You'll find the same rich birria de res, homemade consommé, cheesy fillings, fresh toppings, and satisfying sides like rice, beans, and mac & cheese.

S. Presa St - Classic & Focused

This location keeps it tight and classic with:

  • Focused core birria menu
  • Birria Mac & Cheese $7.99
  • Aguas Frescas & Mexican Sodas $3.50
  • Great for first-timers and quick cravings

Perfect if you want the signature tacos, ramen, or grilled cheese with no distractions.

S. Flores St (y más) – Expanded Street-Style Menu

This location lives up to the "y más" with more variety:

  • Mini Tacos (5 for $10)
    With meats like Buche, Trompo, Tripa, Chorizo, Lengua (+$2), and more
  • Street Tacos (Flour/Corn) $4.00 each
  • Quesadillas Chicas $5 / Grandes $10
  • Frijoles Charros & Compuestos $2–$4
  • Larger Party Tray Option Up to 40 Mini Tacos
  • Aguas Frescas $4.00

This location gives you more ways to enjoy birria and explore traditional street taco flavors.

So, Which Should You Visit?

  • Go S. Presa if you're craving the classic lineup fast
  • Go S. Flores if you want more variety, extra meats, and full-on taco stand vibes

Either way, Birria Barrio delivers big on flavor—with juicy birria, crispy tortillas, and that rich consomé that ties it all together.
