Our Story

Lillo's Tomato Pies is a Hainesport, NJ staple, serving up authentic Trenton-style tomato pies and legendary 16oz ribeye cheesesteaks. Founded on a passion for bold, honest flavors, Lillo's has become a destination for locals and travelers alike. Every pie is crafted with tradition, every cheesesteak is loaded with quality, and every visit is a taste of New Jersey's best. Come for the pies, stay for the flavor—and see why Lillo's is a true icon in the Garden State.

Discover Out and About Burgers

A Local Gem with a Big Bite

Out and About Burgers isn't just a burger stand; it's a drive-thru experience that has captured the hearts (and taste buds) of locals and foodies from afar. With its simple, no-fuss menu and unbeatable prices, this family-run food truck proves you don't need a fancy location to serve unforgettable food. Located in a U-Haul parking lot, this burger joint delivers In-N-Out vibes—with a loyal fan base that might even say it's better.

Quality That Surpasses Fast Food Expectations

What sets Out and About Burgers apart is the quality of its ingredients. From juicy patties and fresh veggies to hand-cut fries and creamy shakes, each item is made to order and packed with flavor. The Cagney sauce adds a unique signature touch that regulars rave about. Whether it's a classic cheeseburger or the customer-favorite double with bacon, the taste is consistently crave-worthy.

Why Out and About Burgers Has Built a Cult Following

For many, a visit to Out and About Burgers is more than a meal—it's a ritual. Despite having no dine-in area and only drive-thru service, the spot sees long lines daily. That's because customers know they're getting fast service, great food, and unbeatable value. With items like grilled cheese, sweet potato fries, malts, and even gluten-free buns, there's something for everyone.

What's So Special About In-N-Out Burger?

In-N-Out is famous for its fresh ingredients, consistency, and secret menu. But for fans of Out and About Burgers, the comparison often tips in favor of the local hero. Why? Many say it's the perfectly crispy onion rings, the generous portions, and the addicting Cagney's Way fries that edge out the competition. Plus, there's a charm in supporting a family-owned business that consistently delivers top-tier meals.

Drive-Thru Simplicity with High-End Flavor

Out and About Burgers keeps things efficient and customer focused. The drive-thru-only model means you can grab a high-quality meal without leaving your car. It's perfect for a quick lunch, a late-night craving, or feeding the whole family affordably. The line might look long, but the staff keep it moving fast—and always with a smile.

The Verdict: Why Locals Keep Coming Back

Whether you're a first-time visitor or a regular who knows the menu by heart, Out and About Burgers never disappoints. The reviews speak for themselves: excellent service, hot and fresh food, and flavors that satisfy on every visit. If you're anywhere near the Puyallup area, this burger spot is a must-try.
