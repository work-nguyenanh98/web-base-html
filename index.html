Malorie ★★★★★

Loved it, had them three days ago and the birria tacos were delicious and the cooks made them in a timely manner when they're were many ppl in line ahead of me. The presentation of the tacos were not messy and how they were set up to go was convenient. I will definitely be returning.

Aaliyah Benavides ★★★★★

The food was so good with reasonable prices. Me and my friend got the queso birria. We got it to go and when we finally got to our hotel at 1am, we sat without anything to each other and just ate the whole thing then passed out peacefully. We slept so good tht night. 10/10

SpiffyG ★★★★★

Don't let the slight look of surly contempt on the guy's face who takes your order put you off. He expresses his love for you through his incredible birria. Aliya Sayani ★★★★★

I've never had Birria tacos in San Antonio so I don't have anywhere to measure em against but these were seriously amazing. They also give you some kind of creamy orange sauce/salsa situation that was so good. I will definitely be coming back here! Also I think 3 tacos for $12 is a great deal
