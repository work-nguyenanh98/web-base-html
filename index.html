Our Story

Mariscos KC is a top-rated seafood restaurant in Kansas City offering bold Mexican flavors with a fresh coastal twist. Known for their generous portions, flavorful seafood boils, and crowd-pleasing favorites like the San Pedro Shrimp Tray and Molcajete de Pulpo, this local gem has earned rave reviews from food lovers and local guides alike. With two convenient locations (in Olathe and Shawnee), Mariscos KC welcomes diners into a vibrant, family-friendly atmosphere that mixes authentic recipes with a fun, social vibe. The service is consistently praised, and the menu offers something for everyone—from seafood towers and Baja tacos to spicy calamari and creamy mango cheesecake. Whether you're here for a casual meal, a birthday celebration, or a night out with friends, Mariscos KC delivers a memorable dining experience that keeps customers coming back. Discover why it's one of Kansas City's most recommended Mexican seafood spots.

Why Choose Mariscos KC?

A Seafood Feast with a Mexican Soul

At Mariscos KC, seafood lovers are treated to an experience that fuses the rich culinary traditions of Mexico with the heartiness of a classic seafood boil. From shrimp ceviche to octopus tacos and massive trays of crab and mussels, every dish is packed with authentic spices and zesty marinades. The sauces, especially their signature house blend, elevate every bite. Whether you go for the seafood fried rice, Baja shrimp tacos, or Cajun pasta, the menu offers incredible variety and bold flavor in every bite. Fresh ingredients, creative recipes, and quality preparation make this more than just a seafood spot—it's a celebration of Mexican coastal cooking.

Why Mariscos KC is a Must-Visit Spot for Seafood in Kansas City

With two well-loved locations, Mariscos KC has become a destination for seafood enthusiasts across the metro area. What sets it apart is the consistent quality, festive environment, and generous portions that satisfy both flavor and value. Locals appreciate the lively ambiance, vibrant music, and thoughtful service. Reviewers consistently mention the fresh taste of their signature dishes like the San Pedro Shrimp Tray, Shrimp Boil, and the Shrimp Seafood Tower. The portions are often described as large enough to share or take home, and the variety appeals to both adventurous eaters and comfort food lovers alike. It's a rare place where families, date nights, and friend groups all feel equally welcome.

Flavorful Favorites Like the San Pedro Shrimp Tray and Seafood Tower Keep Guests Coming Back

The San Pedro Shrimp Tray is one of the most praised dishes at Mariscos KC, noted for its size, seasoning, and succulent shrimp. Served hot and full of bold spices, it often becomes the main reason customers return. Another star on the menu is the Shrimp Seafood Tower, which layers fresh shrimp with flavorful toppings, sauces, and garnishes. Whether you're craving something spicy, buttery, or citrusy, these dishes are packed with flavor and personality. Add in refreshing sides like ceviche, guacamole, or mango cheesecake, and you've got a meal that satisfies from start to finish.

The Service and Atmosphere at Mariscos KC Add to the Appeal

Beyond the food, Mariscos KC is known for its upbeat atmosphere and dedicated staff. From large groups celebrating birthdays to families on a weeknight dinner run, customers repeatedly highlight the friendly service and attention to detail. Servers like Leysi, Yasmin, and Pablo have been called out by name for making guests feel welcome and taken care of. Even during peak hours, food is served quickly and with a smile. The décor is colorful and energetic, and the presence of live music at times adds to the festive vibe. It's a casual yet vibrant setting perfect for seafood lovers looking to enjoy good food and great company.

What You Should Know Before Visiting Mariscos KC

To make the most of your visit, it's smart to come early—especially on weekends when the dining room fills up fast. Reservations are recommended for larger parties. The Olathe location is cozier, while the Shawnee branch offers more spacious seating. Parking can be tight during busy hours but is generally available. Prices range from $10 to $50+ depending on your order, with value-packed combos and shareable dishes making it budget-friendly for groups. Though some reviewers mention inconsistencies in service or flavors on rare occasions, the overwhelming majority praise the quality, freshness, and portion sizes. Overall, Mariscos KC stands out as one of the best seafood destinations in the region

Explore the Mariscos KC Menu and Taste the Variety

Whether you're in the mood for creamy Cajun pasta, tangy calamari, grilled fish, or sizzling fajitas, Mariscos KC offers an expansive menu that caters to every taste. Highlights include the Seafood Boil (with options like shrimp, mussels, and crab legs), the Molcajete de Pulpo (a spicy octopus dish served in a volcanic stone bowl), and an assortment of tacos and tostadas. There are even lighter options and desserts like mango cheesecake. Drinks range from micheladas and fruity cocktails to margarita flights, making it a great place to unwind. From bold spices to fresh seafood, every item reflects the restaurant's passion for flavor and quality.
